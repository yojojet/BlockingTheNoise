For years, too much nitrogen fell on Dutch land, but recently this has become a legislative problem. In 2019 because of a statement from “de raad van staten”, the judge decided that the government didn’t do enough to protect nature. That’s why no more nitrogen is allowed to be emitted in the Netherlands. Building more roads, more houses, bigger barns and expanding airports lead to more nitrogen in that air. That’s why these projects are all on hold.
